    Introduction

    Note: Use the Table of Contents present at the bottom of the channel, or in the pinned comments, to quickly navigate through this channel and find the information you are looking for.

    ​

    Some general notes:

    The game runs on an internal tick system: 1t (tick) = 0.6s (seconds).

    The minimum time between abilities is called the global cooldown: 1 gcd = 3t = 1.8s.

    Auto attacks will only give adrenaline if they hit (splash = no adrenaline gain).

    Debuff spells like Vuln or Enfeeble or Smoke Cloud do not given adrenaline.

    Some useful sheets and resources are given below.

    Vulnerability
    Cooldown: Based on Weapon Speed Fastest (Wand): 4t (2.4s) Average (Staff): 6t (3.6s) Increases damage dealt by 10% Lasts 60s

    Incite Fear
    Cooldown: Based on Weapon Speed Fastest (Wand): 4t (2.4s) Average (Staff): 6t (3.6s) Every ability cast applies a stack: Caps at 5 stacks, and at max stacks uses no runes on ability casts Stacks are lost every 20s, refreshed upon trying to apply another stack Each stack reduces Tsunami's cast cost by -12% adrenaline Frost Surge: At 5 stacks, ability casts trigger Frost Surge; deals 10-50% ability damage Triggering it costs runes even at max stacks Frost Surge is a 5x5 AoE around your primary target which hits up to 8 extra targets in range for 10-50% ability damage each Frost Surge has a 12s cooldown, after an ability cast can trigger it again

    ​

    Exsanguinate
    Cooldown: Based on Weapon Speed Fastest (Wand): 4t (2.4s) Average (Staff): 6t (3.6s) Every ability cast applies a stack: Caps at 12 stacks, and at max stacks uses no runes on ability casts Stacks are lost every 20s, refreshed upon trying to apply another stack Each stack increases basic ability damage by +1% (excludes: and ) Wrack and Ruin : At 12 stacks becomes It deals 60-300% ability damage (120-360% on stun/bound) Resets all stacks and makes your next within 10s do 2 additional hits

    ​

    Smoke Cloud
    Cooldown: Based on Weapon Speed Fastest (Wand): 4t (2.4s) Average (Staff): 6t (3.6s) Increases damage dealt with critical hits by 15% for Magic, 6% otherwise Increases critical hit damage cap by 12% for , 4.8% otherwise Stacks with : makes hitcap 16.8k for , 15.72k otherwise Lasts 120s

    Animate Dead
    Applies a flat damage reduction: Reduction based on 10% of equipped magic tank gear's armour + 33% of your current Defence level after any buffs or debuffs Damage reduced cannot exceed 75% of the damage taken 75% cap is based off the damage after all other damage reductions such as deflect prayers ( / / ) on the hit Lasts 12 minutes Does not reduce / HP / adrenaline gain A calculator can be found here

    Basics

    Combust
    Cooldown: 25t (15s) It is a Burn: Not affected by or Hits every 2t (1.2s), for a total of 5 times Doubles damage of each hit when walked Affected by : Max increases by 20% per rank Walking only causes 50% more damage Two extra hits if used within 10 seconds after Wrack and Ruin Affected by modified by Kerapac's wrist wraps : Next to hit within 10t of the cast will have remaining hits instantly hit at 25% more damage Within 10t means: if is cast t0, has to hit within t0-9 Latest you can cast is t7

    ​

    Corruption Blast
    Cooldown: 25t (15s) It is a DoT: Not affected by or Hits every 2t (1.2s), for a total of 5 times It is an AoE: Spreads to targets within 2 tiles each hit Will not draw aggro of any additional targets

    ​

    Impact
    Cooldown: 25t (15s) Shares cooldown with Stuns and binds for 2t (1.2s) It is affected by : Increases Max by 40% per rank Increases Minimum by 8% per Rank No longer stuns or binds

    ​

    Shock
    Cooldown: 25t (15s) Shares cooldown with Stuns and binds for 2t (1.2s) It causes Knockback Pushes 1x1 targets back 1 tile

    ​

    Surge
    Cooldown: 34t (20.4s) It is a Mobility ability: Moves you forward 10 tiles Affected by Mobile Reduces cooldown to 17t (10.2s) Removes adrenaline gain Can be upgraded with a Double Surge codex: Gives you a second on its own 34t (17t ) internal cooldown that is used after the first

    ​

    Dragon Breath
    Cooldown: 17t (10.2s) It is an AoE: AoE is straight line to target + 3x3 around it Hits 3 targets, inclusive of main It is affected by Kerapac's wrist wraps : Next to hit within 10t of the cast will have remaining hits instantly hit at 25% more damage Within 10t means: if is cast t0, has to hit within t0-9 Latest you can cast is t7 It is affected by the Dragonrider Amulet Boosts damage by 10% Has a 10% chance of applying a 3-hit DoT, each for 10% of max hit, rounded down (excludes boosts that do not normally boost bleeds)

    ​

    Sonic Wave
    Cooldown: 9t (5.4s) Shares cooldown with / Provides a 6% hitchance buff Works cross-style Does not work cross-target

    ​

    Greater Sonic Wave
    Cooldown: 9t (5.4s) All things apply Abilities cast within 4.2s after it gain +100 ability damage

    ​

    Concentrated Blast
    Cooldown: 9t (5.4s) Shares cooldown with / Provides a passive critical hit buff: 5% increased crit chance for each hit it does Lost when swapping mainhands Channelled ability Hits a maximum of 3 times over 5t (3s)

    ​

    Greater Concentrated Blast
    Cooldown: 9t (5.4s) Shares cooldown with / Same critical strike buff as normal Conc Blast Channelled ability with shortened duration Hits 3 times within 3t (1.8s)

    ​

    Chain
    Cooldown: 17t (10.2s) It is an AoE: Hits within a 5 square radius from SW tile of initial target Hits 3 targets, inclusive of main Does not hit things on initial target's SW tile Affected by Caroming hits Caroming rank more targets

    ​

    Greater Chain
    Cooldown: 17t (10.2s) All things apply Secondary targets hit by take the next ability cast on your main target within 6s at 50% of its damage range. and do not get reduced.

    ​

    Wrack
    Cooldown: 5t (3s) Damage boosted on stun/bound targets: Becomes a 52%-182% basic Affected by : Gain ( Rank * 0.6) more adrenaline Damage range is now 0.8 * original damage range

    ​

    Wrack and Ruin
    Cooldown: 5t (3s) Significantly stronger variant of regular Wrack Damage increases to 60%-300% Usable after 12 stacks Damage boosted on stun/bound targets: Becomes a 120%-360% basic Affected by : Gain ( Rank * 0.6) more adrenaline Damage range is now 0.8 * original damage range All stacks lost after using

    ​

    Magma Tempest /
    Cooldown: 25t (15s) Is a 5x5 area-of-effect Hits every 1.2s (2t) over 9.6s for a total of 8 hits Cannot critical hit Has two variants, see differences using !magma

    Thresholds

    Wild Magic
    Cooldown: 34t (20.4s) Two separate, strong hits

    ​

    Asphyxiate
    Cooldown: 34t (20.4s) Stuns for 6t (3.6s) Channelled ability, 4 hits at full channel duration (7t, 4.2s)

    ​

    Deep Impact
    Cooldown: 25t (15s) Shares cooldown with Stuns and bind for 6t (3.6s) Affected by Increases Max by 40% per rank Increases Minimum by 8% per rank No longer stuns or binds

    ​

    Horror
    Cooldown: 25t (15s) Shares cooldown with Deep Impact Stun and binds for 6t (3.6s) Causes Knockback Pushes 1x1 targets back 1 tile

    ​

    Smoke Tendrils
    Cooldown: 75t (45s) Channelled ability, 4 hits at full channel duration (7t, 4.2s) Inflicts self-damage each hit Each hit is a guaranteed critical hit

    ​

    Detonate
    Cooldown: 50t (30s) It is an AoE: It is 3x3 at <100% Charge It is 5x5 at 100% Charge It is affected by Blast Diffusion Boots : Takes 4t (2.4s) to reach 100% instead of 6t (3.6s) Gains 40% charge at a time instead of 20%

    Special Attacks

    Guthix Staff
    Adrenaline Cost: 25% (22.5% with ) Applies an affinity and defence reduction: +2 affinity Reduces level by 5% of base

    ​

    Armadyl Battlestaff
    Adrenaline Cost: 50% (45% with ) Channelled ability, 5 hits at full channel duration (5t, 3s)

    ​

    Fractured Staff of Armadyl
    Special Attack: Instability Cooldown: 100t (60s) Adrenaline Cost: 50% (45% with ) Lasts 50t (30s) Whenever a magic critical hit is inflicted on primary target, a magic auto-attack will also be fired Uses your current selected spell If a staff is not equipped, the auto-attack will come from mainhand weapon Auto-attacks can trigger additional auto-attacks if they land as a critical hit on the primary target For more information on how to use the special attack, use !fsoa in #bot-commands

    Ultimates

    Sunshine
    Cooldown: 100t (60s) Shares a cooldown with It is an on cast buff Increases damage by 50% from t0t51 (52t/31.2s duration); buff starts t1 7x7 buff radius Does not incur the auto cooldown Affected by Planted Feet Duration becomes t0t63 (64t/38.4s duration); buff starts t1

    ​

    Greater Sunshine
    Cooldown: 100t (60s) Shares a cooldown with Duration is 1 tick longer than , t0t64 (65t/39s duration) Using does not extend this further retains the DoT effect of Using will cancel this

    ​

    Metamorphosis
    Cooldown: 100t (60s) Shares a cooldown with Sunshine It is an on hit buff For buff to work, spell type of and abilities and autos must match E.g., Fire into Fire ability works, Fire into Air ability would not Increases damage by 63.5% from t0-t25 (26t/15.6s duration); buff starts t1 Does not incur the auto cooldown

    ​

    Omnipower
    Cooldown: 50t (30s) Affected by the Igneous Kal-Mej and Igneous Kal-Zuk Adrenaline cost becomes 60% Fires 4 hits Increases average ability damage from 300% to 540%

    ​

    Tsunami
    Cooldown: 100t (60s) It is an AoE Provides a Critical Adrenaline buff: For the next 30s, critical hits give 10% adrenaline This buff is applied on release of the ability Adrenaline cost can be reduced through auto-casting Incite Fear Each stack reduces cost by 12$, down to 40% at 5 stacks
