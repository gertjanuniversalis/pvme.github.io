    Introduction

    Note: Use the Table of Contents present at the bottom of the channel, or in the pinned comments, to quickly navigate through this channel and find the information you are looking for.

    ​

    Some general notes:

    The game runs on an internal tick system: 1t (tick) = 0.6s (seconds).

    The minimum time between abilities is called the global cooldown: 1 gcd = 3t = 1.8s.

    Some useful sheets and resources are given below.

    Basics

    Necromancy
    Cooldown: 3t (1.8s) Affected by : Gain ( Rank * 0.1) more adrenaline NOT affected by NOT affected by Automatically activated when in combat (toggleable) Generates 1 Death Spark stack if Omni Guard is equipped At 6 Death Spark stacks the will deal double damage
    Touch of Death
    Cooldown: 24t (14.4s) Generates 4 stacks
    Soul Sap
    Cooldown: 9t (5.4s) Generates 1 stack

    Thresholds

    Thresholds in Necromancy are not like other styles'. Thresholds in Necromancy do not require an adrenaline amount more than the ability's requirement, and drain adrenaline by the shown amount on the tooltip. Some Necromancy Thresholds do not require adrenaline at all but instead will consume stacks of varying types.

    Soul Strike
    No Cooldown Requires minimum 1 stack. One stack is consumed on use. Stuns and Binds for 5t (3s) Deals a reduced amount of damage to enemies within 1 tile of target
    Volley of Souls
    No Cooldown Requires minimum 2 stacks. All stacks consumed on use.
    Finger of Death
    No Cooldown Base adrenaline cost of 60% Adrenaline cost reduced by 10% for each stack Consumes up to 6 stacks
    Bloat
    No Cooldown Base adrenaline cost of 20% Applies Bloated debuff to target for 10 hits Bloated: 25% of initial damage per hit every 5t (3s) On Death: Applies Bloated to up to 9 enemies within 1 tile for 4 hits
    Spectral Scythe
    Cooldown: 25t (15s) Base adrenaline cost of 10% AoE damage in a cone (max 9 targets) On-hit: 25% chance to generate 1 stack Able to be re-cast upon unlock from the Necromancy skill tree: 2nd cast costs 20% and does AoE within 2 tiles of you (max 9 targets) Also has a 25% chance to generate a stack 3rd cast costs 30% and does AoE within 2 tiles of you (max 9 targets) Damage is increased 1% for per 1% mission life points on target
    Blood Siphon
    Cooldown: 75t (45s) No adrenaline cost AoE to enemies within 2 tiles of player Channeled attack: 4 hits over 8t (4.8s) Completing the full channel deals 100% of the total heal plus extra damage to the target Heal for 70% of the damage dealt

    Ultimates

    Death Skulls
    Cooldown: 100t (60s) Launchs a Death Skull that bounces up to 4 times after hitting the target Prioritizes enemies by maximum life points, but can also bounce off of you The Necromancy Zuk Cape reduces the adrenaline cost of thie ability to 60% and also adds two additional bounces
    Living Death
    Cooldown: 150t (90s) Change forms into Death form, empowering Necromancy abilities for 30s On-cast: the cooldowns of and are reset While active: generates 2 stacks generates an addition 6% adrenaline deals 1.5x damage cooldown reduced to 12s

    Conjures and Commands

    Conjure Skeleton Warrior
    No Cooldown 70t (42s) duration Conjure a Skeleton warrior: Skeleton attacks every 5t (3s) Generates 1 Rage stack with each attack Deals an additional 1% ability damage per Rage stack
    Command Skeleton Warrior
    Cooldown: 25t (15s) Commands a conjured skeleton to perform rapid hits (every 2t (1.2s)) for 10t (6s)
    Conjure Vengeful Ghost
    No Cooldown Base adrenaline cost of 30% 50t (30s) duration Conjure a Vengeful Ghost: Ghost attacks every 7t (4.2s) Heal for 175% of the damage dealt by the ghost
    Command Vengeful Ghost
    No Cooldown Base adrenaline cost of 70% Applies Haunted to the target Haunted: Takes 2x damage from all attack capped at 35% ability bonus damage
    Conjure Putrid Zombie
    Cooldown: 50t (30s) 75t (45s) duration Conjure a Putrid Zombie: Zombie attacks every 6t (3.6s) Zombie inflicts poison damage every 3t (1.8s) to enemies within 1 tile
    Command Putrid Zombie
    No Cooldown Command the Zombie to explode, dealing large damage to enemies within 2 tiles

    Special Attacks

    Death Grasp //
    Cooldown: 50t (30s) Base adrenaline cost of 25% Stuns and Binds the target for 8t (4.8s) Deals extra damage for each stack held, but also consumes all stacks The spec seems to hitcap easily without many stacks, so consider using if at high stacks before using the Death Grasp as to not waste stacks
    Death Essence
    Cooldown: 100t (60s) Base adrenaline cost of 50% 50t (30s) duration On-cast: immediately readies Death Spark Death Spark causes to deal double damage For the duration, , , and immediately ready Death Spark

    Incantations

    Lesser Bone Shield
    No Cooldown Toggleable bone shield with a tier matching 25% TODO;necromancy; level Allows the use of non-offensive abilities that typically require a shield Rune cost: 5 5
    Threads of Fate
    Cooldown: 100t (60s) 8t (4.8s) duration necromancy attacks will also be cast on up to 4 additional enemies within 4 tiles of the target Rune cost: 5 2 1
    Greater Bone Shield
    No Cooldown Toggleable bone shield with a tier matching 50% TODO;necromancy; level Allows the use of non-offensive abilities that typically require a shield Rune cost: 10 10 5
    Life Transfer
    Cooldown: 75t (45s) Deals self damage equal to 50% max health Extends the duration of all active spirits by 34t (20.4s) Rune cost: 10 5 2 1
    Invoke Death
    Cooldown: 6t (3.6s) 10t (6s) duration Your next necromancy attack during the 10t duration applies Death Mark If your target drops below 20% hp, the next necromancy attack to hit it will execute it. Damage caps at 30,000 Rune cost: 10 5 2 2
    Darkness
    No cooldown 400t (4 min) duration Applies the Darkness aspect to self 20% chance to dodge incoming attacks Rune cost: 30 15 8 4
    Aspect of Evasion: You may only have one aspect active at once
    Split Soul
    Cooldown: 100t (60s) 34t (20.4s) duration 400% of the healing from soul split will instead be dealt as damage Rune cost: 10 5 2 1
