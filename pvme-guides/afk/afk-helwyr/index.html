    Magic AFK Method (~43-50 kph)

    Courtesy of #Unknown user, #Mia Groves and #Unknown user

    Overview

    Mandatory AFK Requirements
    The following ARE REQUIRED for this method to work: Cutting corners will result in failure A more extensive list can be found in #AFK Overview:
    Items
    T88+ Elite Seasinger dual wield Wen Book or Ful Book Essence of Finality At least 3 pieces of Cryptbloom armour Cinderbane Gloves
    Other
    Animate Dead Prism of Restoration Weapon poison +++) Greater Concentrated Blast Blood Reaver Use scrolls with autofire set to 10 Aura, in order of effectiveness

    Note: Depending on the setup used, you may need to up the autofire rate for your blood reaver.

    ​

    Standard Setup

    Positioning

    Stand 1 step north-east of the mushroom directly south of the center mushroom.

    Action Bar

    Note: if is not owned, kills are possible but significantly less consistent. You will need to remove to make it work.

    Ranged AFK Method (~56-58 kph)

    Courtesy of #Saltea, #I O and #Unknown user

    ​

    Overview

    Mandatory AFK Requirements
    The following ARE REQUIRED for this method to work: Cutting corners will result in failure A more extensive list can be found in #AFK Overview:
    Items
    Igneous Kal-Xil Portent of restoration X If you run out of these due to bad rng, bank for more for safety. Wen Book Essence of Finality Cinderbane Gloves At least a Noxious longbow Splintering arrows
    Other
    Prism of Restoration Weapon poison +++) Greater ricochet Blood Reaver Use scrolls with autofire set to 15 (increase the speed if you are dying) Vampyrism aura

    For fastest kill speeds, a Bow of the Last Guardian should be used. A Seren Godbow or Noxious Bow are slower substitutes.

    Can add tank perks to armour for increased consistency (Crystal shield 4 and Absorbative 4 )

    ​

    Standard Setup

    Action Bar

    Note: To reduce the usage of portents of restoration, at the expense of increasing killtimes move debilitate further up the bar

    Positioning

    Stand 1 step north-east of the mushroom directly south of the center mushroom.
