    Kerapac, the Bound: Normal Mode

    Low Effort Ranged Normal Mode Kerapac Guide (~25kph)

    Courtesy of #doge, #Unknown user, #Unknown user, and #Unknown user

    ​

    Explanation

    This method will reliably allow you to get consistent Kerapac (NM) kills without needing much input or effort. It is recommended to have a slayer task (with slayer helm on your Anachronia slayer lodge stand) when using this method, but it is not mandatory.

    ​

    If using the low-level setup, you must use a Prism of Restoration and a vulnerability bomb at the start of each kill.

    Set Blood Reaver's autofire rate to 5.

    ​

    If using the high-level setup, vulnerability bombs are optional, and Prism of Restoration is used when you repot.

    Set Blood Reaver's autofire rate to 30.

    ​

    This method requires using Bik arrows

    BoLG > SGB / Hex > Nox

    ​

    Make sure to loot quickly, as Surge , and Dive will keep you in combat, interfering with Persistant Rage

    Prism of Restoration will also keep you in combat.

    ​

    Mandatory AFK Requirements

    The following ARE REQUIRED for this method to work: Cutting corners will result in failure
    Items
    Essence of Finality Weapon poison +++ Cinderbane Gloves Scripture of wen Weapon with Caroming 4 Bik arrows Elder overload salve Vulnerability bomb
    Passives/Familiar
    Soulsplit Anguish or Desolation Blood reaver with scrolls Set autofire rate to 5 if using low-level setup. Set autofire rate to 30 if using high-level setup. Persistent rage Vampyrism aura
    Others
    Greater Ricochet Prism of Restoration Auto-retaliate is ON

    Low Effort Magic Normal Mode Kerapac Guide (~22kph)

    Courtesy of #FriggityFrank and #cue decs

    ​

    Explanation

    This method will reliably allow you to get consistent Kerapac (NM) kills without needing much input or effort. Consistency and safety are attained through a heavy focus on maximising damage dealt while minimising damage taken. It is strongly recommended to have a slayer task (with slayer helm on your Anachronia slayer lodge stand) when using this method.

    ​

    You should be using Soulsplit and Torment / Affliction for the entire fight.

    If using Affliction , you must either use Powder of Penance or click an Ancient Elven Ritual Shard at the start of each kill.

    ​

    This method requires using at least a Seismic Wand and Animate Dead with some tank armour , as raw damage is the focus and this setup is not as strong on healing when compared to the other styles' setups.

    ​

    Aura Choice

    Auras:

    For once, Maniacal is actually your best choice for your aura here. Usage of Animate Dead and Cryptbloom armour heavily mitigates the downsides that this aura usually presents, and the extra damage makes up for the more defensive setup.

    Dark Magic provides extra damage and poison procs, but the lack of raw damage may be detrimental for phases where Sunshine isn't active.

    ​

    Mandatory AFK Requirements

    The following ARE REQUIRED for this method to work: Cutting corners will result in failure
    Items
    Essence of Finality Weapon poison +++ Cinderbane Gloves Scripture of wen At least a Seismic wand At least 3 pieces of Cryptbloom armour Igneous Kal-Mej or Igneous Kal-Zuk Elder overload salve Vulnerability bomb
    Passives/Familiar
    Soulsplit Torment or Affliction Ripper demon with scrolls Persistent rage Maniacal aura or Dark magic aura
    Others
    Exsanguinate Animate Dead Auto-retaliate is ON

    ​

    Relics and Passives

    This method strongly relies on the use of the following passive effects. Missing any of these may harm the effectiveness of this method.

    Fury of the Small

    Conservation of Energy

    Adrenaline Retention (Persistent Rage / Adrenaline Urn / Stall )

    Ring of Vigour Passive

    Upgraded Pontifex Shadow Ring Passive

    Nodon Dragonkin Task

    Method/Setup

    Upon entering the boss instance, Surge 2x, then walk forward one tile. Make sure you have all buffs/prayers active, and queue Smoke Cloud . Once you begin auto-retaliating, throw a Vulnerability Bomb during your first Greater Concentrated Blast and AFK from there. Once the kill ends, gather your loot and teleport to War's Retreat. Restore prayer and heal at the bank if needed, restore adrenaline to 91% or more, and repeat.

    ​

    Low Effort Melee Normal Mode Kerapac Guide (~22kph)

    Courtesy of #FriggityFrank

    Explanation

    This method will reliably allow you to get consistent Kerapac (NM) kills without needing much input or effort. Consistency and safety are attained through a heavy focus on maximising the Blood Reaver familiar's potential, utilizing weapon poison, Abyssal Parasite stacks, and many sources of healing. It is strongly recommended to have a slayer task (with slayer helm on your Anachronia slayer lodge stand) when using this method.

    ​

    You should be using Soulsplit and Turmoil / Malevolence for the entire fight.

    If using Malevolence , you must either use Powder of Penance or click an Ancient Elven Ritual Shard at the start of each kill.

    ​

    This method requires using an Abyssal Scourge and Jaws of the Abyss , as the action bar requires the extra adrenaline.

    ​

    Aura Choice

    Auras:

    Vampyrism provides extra safety through its healing and provides more poison damage via Blood Reaver passive ability. Due to the constant damage being taken while fighting Kerapac, this is by far the safest and most reliable aura choice to afk with.

    Do not use Berserker for afking, as it will get you killed very easily.

    ​

    Mandatory AFK Requirements

    The following ARE REQUIRED for this method to work: Cutting corners will result in failure
    Items
    Essence of Finality Weapon poison +++ Cinderbane Gloves Scrimshaw of vampyrism Abyssal scourge Jaws of the Abyss Igneous Kal-Ket or Igneous Kal-Zuk Elder overload salve Vulnerability bomb
    Passives/Familiar
    Soulsplit Turmoil or Malevolence Blood reaver with scrolls Set autofire rate to 15-20. Persistent rage Vampyrism aura
    Others
    Nodon dragonkin task Slayer helmet stand Access to the Altar of War Instantly heals your familiar. Auto-retaliate is ON
