    Misc Slayer Guides

    Introduction

    This guides cover the monsters that can be killed on a Creatures of Daemonheim cluster task. The creatures that can be killed on this task are: Frostdragon, Kal'Gerion demon, Seeker, Soulgazer, Edimmu, or monsters in Dungeoneering floors. Currently the guide contains guides for Kal'Gerion demons, Soulgazers, and Edimmus.

    ​
