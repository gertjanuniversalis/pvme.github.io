    Metal Dragons

    Metal Dragons

    ​

    Introduction

    There are 6 types of metal dragons: bronze, iron, steel, mithril, adamant, and rune. All except the bronze dragons can be assigned by either Laniakea or Mandrith. This guide will discuss the five that can be assigned.

    ​

    Currently there are only methods detailed for Adamant and Rune dragons. If you have a method for any of the others please make a post in #suggestions.

    ​

    Useful Items and unlocks

    Items
    Elder overload salve) Acts as in one, also helps with upkeep. Dragon slayer gloves Increases EXP per kill and accuracy. Kethsi Ring Provides a 4% damage boost towards metal dragons, making it the BIS ring to use if owned. Metal dragon trinkets Can increase kph, XP per kill or note items; these were not used in testing the method.
    Passives/unlocks
    Dragon slayer (perk)) Increases damage and KPH. Dragon slayer (sigil) Increases damage and KPH if slaying actively. Death Note Useful for AFK looting valuable metal dragon bones.

    Desirable Drops

    All metal dragons drop bones ranging from regular dragon bones to reinforced dragon bones.

    All metal dragons also drop a guaranteed plated salvage corresponding to their type.

    Mithril and Adamant dragons drop Dragon Full Helm - 1/8192* and 1/16384 respectively

    Required for the Slayer log, may alternatively be obtained from the Abomination

    *includes the drop chance from Chewed bones

    Adamant and Rune dragons drop Forcae's Journal pages - 1/20

    20 are required for MQC

    Rune dragons drop Kethsi Ring - 1/1024, 1/512 from elites

    Required for the Slayer log

    Rune dragons drop Kethsi outfit scroll - 1/200 only from elites, 100% from Barry the destroyer

    Required for the Slayer log (only 1 outfit required)

    Rune dragons drop T85 boot upgrades

    Required for the Slayer log, may alternatively be obtained from Elite Dungeons using Lucky Charms

    Rune dragons

    If going for Ultimate slayer, you may need to slay multiple thousand rune dragons before obtaining the Kethsi scroll, dropped only from Elite Rune dragons or Barry the destroyer. Use to their mechanics and phasing, range is the only viable combat style. Fortunately, they don't deal much damage and can be easily AFK'd.

    ​

    Stats

    XP per kill: 2051

    Optimal kills per hour: 185+ (AFK)

    Optimal XP per hour: ~379k (AFK)

    ​

    Location and How to Get There

    Rune dragons are located on Mount Firewake on Kethsi accessible through the World Gate south of the Eagles' Peak lodestone . You can teleport to the World Gate directly with . Remember to attune the portal to Kethsi before entering.

    ​

    Method: Ranged (AFK)

    Stand at the immediate southeast of the southern statue (yellow sqaure) and set up nearby (red square) so you can restock without moving.

    Your main targets are the 2 southern spawns in the picture above. Kill all rune dragons around the statues when you initially arrive the spot to reset their spawn points. For the most part, these 2 dragons should be the only ones targeting you at any time.

    Rune dragon aggression is permanent as long as you're in the area. This is makes it easy to trap stray dragons behind the pillar if the hits them.
