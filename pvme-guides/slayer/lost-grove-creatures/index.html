    Lost Grove Creatures

    Creatures of the Lost Grove

    Introduction

    For a creatures of the lost grove task you can choose between three different monsters: Moss Golems, Vinecrawlers and Bulbous crawlers. Moss Golems are the fastest, best xp/hr and best gp/hr, therefore the other ones are only worth doing if you are looking to get a soul.

    ​

    Desirable Drops

    Cinderbane Gloves - Drop Rate of 1/1500 on task and 1/5000 off task

    Ancient Elven Ritual Shard - Drop Rate of 1/1500 on task and 1/5000 off task

    Torn Grimoire Pages - Drop Rate of 1/512 on task and 1/1536 off task.

    ​

    This makes Lost Grove creatures worth 74,191 in uniques alone, paired with high kph of Moss Golems, this makes them a very good gp task.

    ​

    Location and How to Get There

    Creatures of the Lost Grove can be accessed through the monolith west of the Incandesent wisp colony, west of Yanille.

    ​

    The easiest way to get there would be through the fairy ring (code B J S) after repairing it with 5 Bittercap mushrooms.

    An alternative method is to run west from the Incandescent wisp colony, accessible via the portal in the Max guild garden, the elder divination outfit, or via the fairy ring south of Castle Wars (code B K P).

    ​

    Alternatively, players can also utilise the Solak boss portal through Max Guild, War's Retreat or the teleport through the grouping system. The exact location for each monster can be found on the map in game.

    ​

    Moss Golems

    Stats

    XP per kill : 2951 + 973.8

    XP per kill : 2408.8

    Optimal kills per hour: 960 (700 afk)

    Optimal XP per hour : 2.3M (~1.68M afk)

    ​

    Useful Items

    Items
    Abyssal armour spikes (alloy)) Very strong here if using melee as it allows for increased hits and healing via and . Other armour spikes work as well. Essence of Finality with an Annihilation stored. Provides a 1% damage boost for 30s per stack, capping at 20 stacks. A stack is gained on each successful hit, including armour spikes. Only recommended if the player is using both a Blood nihil familiar and the T99 prayer Malevolence , as your hit chance will drop too low without them and the special attack will clear itself upon splashing.
    Passive/Abillity unlocks
    Restocking Cannon Ability Allows restocking without user input.

    Method and Where to Stand

    Moss Golems have hard hitting melee auto-attacks, and can hit from halberd range. When they lose HP, their heads pop off and spawn a Mossy Rock that also deals damage. To prevent taking too much damage, the Enhanced Devoted and Devoted perks are extremely important for survivability, as you will be praying regardless of which method you are using.

    ​

    Moss Golems can be effectively afk'd with either a melee or magic setup, which will be detailed here.

    ​

    For the respective methods, place the cannon on the red square, and stand on the yellow square shown in the image below. For the melee method, stand in a corner north east of the treeline on the map. For the magic method, the cannon is placed closer to the entrance as you will not have access to the Moss Golems further from the entrance. Placements is also shown in the low effort video example shown below.

    ​

    Melee

    With the melee setup, Devotion ability is a high priority here. For the most part the task can be done afk using the bar below, however make sure to keep an eye out and eat food if required. Moss golems have a high defence level, so usage of a Blood Nihil is recommended. The Vampyrism aura and Scrimshaw of Vampyrism are the main sources of healing, with Alloy Spikes helping with the healing.

    ​

    If using the Corbicula Rex perk from the Ranch out of Time, excess adrenaline can be used in the form of the or , both of these can be rotated between to greatly increase the damage dealt. A Blood Nihil is especially important if using spec, as it loses its effect and duration upon splashing.

    ​

    Magic

    Magic is used via Legacy mode.

    ​

    One should stand on top of a hill near the entrance next to a pile of Moss Golem bodies to channel golems attacking the player onto 1 spot so they can be easily AOE'd. If using the Inquisitor's Staff , Blood Burst can be used for a small damage reduction to save a large amount of blood runes, improving profits. Otherwise, Blood Barrage should be used.

    ​

    3 pieces of magic tank armour (helmet, legs, boots) should be used when maging in legacy to mitigate the damage taken with Animate Dead . If using Cryptbloom armour , only the helm and boots should be needed. As you take significantly less damage with this method, the Penance Aura can be used for the task to be more AFK.
