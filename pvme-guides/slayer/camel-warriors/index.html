    Camel Warriors

    Camel Warriors

    ​

    Introduction

    Camel Warriors are one of the 4 Raptor Mobs, generally killed for the collection log. As such, they should only be killed on task where they are 5x more likely to drop the

    ​

    Camel warriors have one main mechanic - After taking some damage, they split into 3 mirages - Blood, Smoke and Shadow. Each has their own unique abilities. All attack with magic.

    Shadow mirages - Drains points after charging their overhead bar.

    Blood mirages - Heal other mirages for ~900 HP per attack.

    Smoke mirages - Cast the Confuse, Weaken and Snare spells on the player, reducing hitchance and damage by 5%, and freezing you in place for 10s respectively.

    After the mirages have been killed, the camel warrior re-appears with the HP it had before phasing, and can now be finished off.

    ​

    Mirages can be bypassed by dealing a singular hit of more than 20,125 damage, before hitcaps apply.

    For example, if is used on task in with 120 , it will be capped at 15K, but in theory it would've dealt 30K damage, so the camel will not be phased. This will then allow the player to finish off the camel by dealing the remaining 5125 damage in 1 hit.

    If the player fails to deal 5125 damage in the next hit, the camel will phase into mirages. This is why perks such as crackling and aftershock should be avoided when relying on methods that skip the mirage mechanic.

    ​

    All methods suggested below use tricks that bypass the mechanic entirely, as it is very time consuming and detrimental to kph to kill Camel warriors "normally".

    ​

    Stats

    XP per kill : 2186 + 721.3

    XP per kill : 4768.8

    Optimal kills per hour: 300 , 240 , 105 AFK

    Optimal XP per hour: ~1.43M (~500K AFK)

    ​

    Desirable Drops

    Camel Warriors have a valuable loot table, however, due to how slow it is to kill them, they have an underwhelming gp/hr. They are killed for their Slayer log drops:

    Camel Staff (1 / 2,000 on task, 1 / 10,000 off task)

    Cursed Amascut Sand (1 / 1,024)

    The Ugthanatos (1 / 25)

    Raptor key part 3 (1 / 256)

    ​

    Location and How to Get There

    They can be found on the island east of Sophanem, which is accessed via stepping stones (no level needed) near a group of crocodiles. The area is affected by desert heat.

    The quickest way to get there is via travelling south from Pharaoh's sceptre/Sceptre of the Gods option 2 followed by

    Alternatively, one can go via a gate in the eastern wall of Sophanem that allows access from the city.

    ​

    Method: Active Melee [PoSD]

    The fastest way to kill camels relies on doing trips to a player owned dungeon with 3-4 camel warriors, using and trying to kill as many camels as possible within the duration while skipping the mirage mechanic. It also requires essentially best in slot melee gear to be as consistent as possible.

    Method: AFK Cannon

    Courtesy of #mionee and #FishBones

    This method involves not engaging in combat with the Camel Warriors at all, relying on cannon and poison damage which passively kill the Camel Warriors without triggering their mirage mechanic.

    ​

    Preset Suggestion & Breakdown

    Strategy

    Place the in the Camel Warrior island, while staying in the tiny island before the stepping stone, as shown below:

    Players can do other activities in the meantime (e.g. Fletching ).

    To avoid being attacked by camels, players should bring runes for Telekinetic Grab to collect the drops along with using a legendary pet with the "Scavenge" perk active.

    Do NOT call your pet as it will end up being on the same small island as you, and will not able to access the drops.
