    ​

    Introduction and Notes

    This channel covers perk setups, where to put them on your gear, and how to obtain them. For explanations of the perks used, see #perk-information.

    This channel will first list all setups, then perk gizmo combos.

    Insert components in the order listed in the combo.

    ​

    For augments where there are multiple gizmos, gizmos are seperated by + symbol.

    e.g. Precise 6 Aftershock 1 + Aftershock 4 Equilbrium 2 one gizmo with P6AS1, and a second gizmo with AS4E2.

    ​

    X represents a secondary perk to combo with the first perk in a single gizmo.

    e.g. Biting 4 X Biting 4 comboed with a valid perk (like Biting 4 Mobile).

    ​

    If you get Aftershock, you will need it on all your weapons for the style.

    Item Levelling Guide

    Shattered worlds currently remains the fastest and most efficient ways to level up your items. Video examples are given below demonstrating this method. Alternative methods include abyssal demons and other such slayer monsters that can be AFK'd for relatively high combat and invention experience.

    Item Levelling Video Examples
    For more information, use the !shatteredworlds command.
    Shattered Worlds
    Base Tank Evil Lucario

    Note: Use 8 1 (1/1.08 at 120 invention) for Enlightened 4, instead of 8 as mentioned in the video.

    Starter Perks

    Magic Slayer/AoE Weapon Perk Choices
