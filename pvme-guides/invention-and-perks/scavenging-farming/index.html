    Introduction

    Scavenging is an perk which provides players a chance to get uncommon or rare components as a drop, whenever they kill an NPC.

    ​

    Each rank provides a 1% chance per rank for uncommon components to drop from a kill, with a 1% chance that they will be rare components instead.

    Scavenging 4 on level 20 equipment provides a 4.4% chance per kill, of activation, with a 1% chance that the components will be rare instead of uncommon.

    ​

    More information about here: https://runescape.wiki/w/Scavenging

    ​

    Killing monsters en masse for procs is called scav farming, a low effort/AFK way to obtain a variety of components.

    ​
